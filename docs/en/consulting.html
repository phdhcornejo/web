CONSULTING

Strategic advisory

PhD. Hernán Cornejo provides strategic advisory services focused on organizational transformation, strategy design and the development of institutional capabilities in complex environments.

His work bridges academic research and applied practice, integrating organizational theory, strategic management and change processes into high-impact advisory engagements.

Organizational strategy

Design and implementation of strategic frameworks for public, private and nonprofit organizations operating in competitive and uncertain environments.

Change management

Support in organizational transformation processes, addressing resistance to change, cultural adaptation and leadership alignment.

Innovation & digital transformation

Integration of emerging technologies and redesign of organizational processes to enhance adaptability, efficiency and innovation capacity.

Institutional development

Strengthening organizational capabilities in public institutions, universities and international organizations.

Methodological approach

The consulting approach is based on diagnostic analysis, strategic design, implementation support and capability transfer to ensure sustainable organizational change.

Each engagement is adapted to the specific institutional context, prioritizing long-term impact and organizational autonomy.
