BOOKS

Academic and professional publications

PhD. Hernán Cornejo is the author of a series of books focused on organizational strategy, change management, innovation and the challenges of leadership in complex and technology-driven environments.

His work explores emerging paradigms such as artificial intelligence, decentralized systems, quantum decision-making and strategic complexity.
