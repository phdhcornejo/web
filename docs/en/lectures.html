LECTURES

Keynotes and executive programs

Dr. Hernán Cornejo actively participates in international conferences, academic seminars and executive education programs focused on leadership, strategy and organizational transformation.

His lectures combine academic rigor with applied experience in organizational change, innovation and strategic management in complex environments.

Organizational transformation

Keynotes focused on change processes in organizations, addressing resistance to change, organizational culture and adaptive leadership models.

Strategy and innovation

Exploration of strategy as a dynamic process in competitive environments, integrating innovation and emerging management paradigms.

Leadership in complex environments

Development of leadership capabilities in contexts of uncertainty, volatility and accelerated digital transformation.

Artificial intelligence and the future of work

Analysis of how artificial intelligence reshapes decision-making, organizational structures and the nature of work itself.

Digital economy and new organizational models

Study of decentralized organizations, platform ecosystems and emerging forms of digital coordination.

Approach

Lectures are designed to integrate conceptual frameworks, real-world cases and strategic reflection, enabling participants to translate ideas into actionable organizational insights.
