RESEARCH

Research focus areas

The academic work of Dr. Hernán Cornejo focuses on organizational transformation, strategic management and innovation processes in complex and uncertain environments.

His research integrates management sciences, organizational theory and social analysis, with a particular emphasis on Latin America and emerging institutional contexts.

1. Organizational strategy

Analysis of strategic formulation, implementation and evaluation processes in both public and private organizations operating in complex environments.

2. Change management

Study of organizational change dynamics, resistance to change, cultural transformation and adaptive leadership mechanisms.

3. Innovation & digital transformation

Research on the impact of emerging technologies on organizational structures, decision-making processes and institutional performance.

4. Organizational behavior

Exploration of internal organizational dynamics including leadership, motivation, collective learning and capability development.

5. Complexity and institutional development

Study of organizations operating in highly complex environments, focusing on uncertainty, governance and institutional adaptation.

Methodological approach

The research approach combines qualitative and quantitative methods, comparative analysis, case studies and interdisciplinary frameworks aimed at understanding organizational phenomena in depth.
