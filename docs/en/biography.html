BIOGRAPHY

PhD. Hernán Cornejo

PhD. Hernán Cornejo is a university professor, researcher, international consultant and author specializing in organizational strategy, innovation, change management and institutional transformation.

His work is positioned at the intersection of organizational theory and management practice, with a strong focus on transformation processes, institutional learning and the development of strategic capabilities in both public and private organizations.

Throughout his academic and professional career, he has participated in research projects, executive education programs and consulting assignments across Latin America, contributing to the design and analysis of management models oriented toward innovation and competitiveness.

His approach integrates interdisciplinary perspectives from management sciences, social theory and strategic analysis, promoting a critical and applied understanding of contemporary organizational dynamics.

He is the author of books, book chapters and academic articles, and has served as a speaker at international conferences, seminars and executive education programs.

His work is dedicated to the development of more adaptive, reflective and resilient organizations capable of operating in complex and uncertain environments.
